Methodology

Our Professional
Installation Process

Perfection cannot be rushed. Discover the meticulous, multi-stage workflow we deploy for every vehicle that enters our climate-controlled studio.

1

Vehicle Inspection

We begin with a comprehensive evaluation under high-intensity inspection lights. We measure clear coat thickness, map out existing damage, and set realistic expectations for the final result.

2

Surface Preparation

A rigorous decontamination wash using pH-neutral foam, followed by iron particle removers and manual clay bar treatments to strip the paint of all bonded contaminants and old waxes.

3

Paint Correction

Using dual-action polishers and premium compounds, we permanently level the clear coat. This multi-stage process removes swirl marks, holograms, and light scratches, restoring a flawless mirror finish.

4

Protection Installation

Application takes place in our ISO-standard clean room. Whether we are precisely laying Paint Protection Film (PPF) or hand-applying a Ceramic Coating, this stage locks in the corrected perfection.

5

Quality Check

The vehicle undergoes a strenuous final inspection. We use infrared curing lamps to bake coatings and edge-check all PPF to guarantee zero lifting, bubbling, or high spots before delivery.

6

Vehicle Delivery

A final wipe-down is performed. We then invite you to the studio for the final reveal, walking you through the completed work and educating you on long-term maintenance for your newly protected vehicle.
