The Optum Standard

Premium Car Protection

Elevating automotive perfection through cutting-edge technology, imported materials, and artisan craftsmanship. Discover our comprehensive suite of detailing and protection service.

Invisible Shield

Paint Protection Film (PPF)

Our premium TPU Paint Protection Film is the ultimate defense for your vehicle's clear coat. Custom bulk-cut for tucked, invisible edges, it forms a permanent barrier against the harshest elements of the road.

  • Self-Healing Technology Light scratches and swirl marks vanish with heat exposure from the sun or engine.
  • Impact & Scratch Resistance Defends against rock chips, road debris, bug acids, and minor abrasions.
  • Long-Term Paint Protection Prevents UV oxidation and chemical etching, preserving showroom quality for years.

Liquid Glass Finish

Ceramic Coating

A nano-ceramic liquid polymer that chemically bonds with your factory paint, creating a rigid super-slick layer of protection that wax and sealants simply cannot match.

  • Extreme Hydrophobic Properties Water beads instantly and rolls off the surface, making washing effortless.
  • Unmatched Gloss Enhancement Creates a deep, mirror-like "candy apple" shine that permanently enhances the paint.
  • Dirt & Contaminant Resistance Repels brake dust, mud, and road grime, keeping your vehicle cleaner for longer.

Next-Gen Nano Tech

Graphene Coating

The latest evolution in automotive protection. Graphene-infused coatings reduce surface temperature and provide a tighter web of protection compared to traditional ceramics, preventing water spotting.

  • Advanced Heat Resistance Dissipates heat rapidly to prevent water spots from baking into the clear coat.
  • Extended Durability Provides up to 7+ years of resilient, highly chemical-resistant protection.
  • Anti-Static Properties Actively repels dust micro-particles to keep dark-colored vehicles looking pristine.

Surgical Precision

Premium Car Detailing

Far beyond a standard wash. Our restorative detailing processes rejuvenate your vehicle inside and out, bringing it back to—or better than—factory condition.

  • Paint Restoration (Correction) Multi-stage machine compounding to remove heavy swirls, scratches, and oxidation.
  • Exterior Decontamination Clay bar and iron removal treatments to extract embedded industrial fallout.
  • Deep Interior Detailing Steam cleaning, leather conditioning, Alcantara revival, and odor elimination.

Aesthetic Transformation

Vinyl Car Wrapping

Completely transform the look of your vehicle while protecting the OEM paint underneath. We offer hundreds of colors, textures, and finishes from the world's leading vinyl manufacturers.

  • Complete Color Change Switch from gloss to matte, satin, color-shift, or carbon fiber finishes seamlessly.
  • Custom Styling & Accents Chrome deletes, roof wraps, racing stripes, and bespoke custom designs.
  • Light Vinyl Protection Acts as a sacrificial layer against light UV damage, sap, and minor scratches.
